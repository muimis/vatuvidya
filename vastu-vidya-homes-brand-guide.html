Positioning

Two kinds of alignment.

A house is aligned twice — once to the compass, once to the contractor's calendar. Most firms hand you one and leave you to chase the other: a Vastu consultant who reads the plot and disappears, or a builder who pours the foundation without asking which way the kitchen should face. Vastu Vidya Homes holds both lines at once. The same team that reads your plot's geometry stays on site until the keys are in your hand.

"The only Trivandrum firm that treats spatial alignment and construction management as one discipline, not two hand-offs."
01PRECISEWe speak in measurements, not mysticism. Every Vastu reading ties to a drawing, a dimension, an approval.
02GROUNDEDLaterite, teak, monsoon light. We build in Kerala's own materials and calendar, not a borrowed aesthetic.
03DIRECTIONALVastu is a compass, not a constraint. We give a clear reading and a clear next step, always.
04ACCOUNTABLEOne firm, one number to call — from the first sketch to the K-Smart approval stamp.

Clearspace & Misuse

Give it room. Leave it alone.

CLEARSPACE — keep an empty margin equal to the width of the outer V's arm (unit "x") on all sides. Nothing — text, edge, other logos — enters that margin.

MINIMUM SIZE — 24px tall on screen, 18mm tall in print, mark alone. Below that, drop to the wordmark-only lockup.

MISUSE — the five things that break the mark:

Stretched
Off-palette colour
Rotated
Drop shadow / bevel
Crowded / cropped

Grid System

The 9×9 grid behind everything.

NW · AIR NE · WATER SW · EARTH SE · FIRE

The Vastu Purusha Mandala — the 9×9 grid every reading is plotted against — is the brand's structural DNA, not just a diagram in the appendix. The same division shows up as a background watermark on documents, as the crop grid for photography, and as the column structure of this guide. The centre square, the Brahmasthan, stays open in a floor plan and stays quiet in a layout: no headline, no logo, no clutter sits dead-centre.

  • NE Ishanya — water, worship spaces, kept light and open
  • SE Agneya — fire, kitchen placement
  • SW Nairutya — earth, master bedroom, the home's ballast
  • NW Vayavya — air, guest rooms, movement

Imagery Direction

Material honesty over stock-photo gloss.

Shoot the actual site, the actual client, the actual hands doing the work — never a stand-in villa render. Low, warm, late-afternoon Kerala light. Natural materials left visible: exposed laterite, unpainted teak, wet concrete. One human figure in most site photographs, for scale and for proof someone is actually there.

Voice & Tone

Confident, not mystical.

We're the expert in the room, so we don't need to oversell. State the reading, state the reasoning, state the next step. Warmth comes from specificity — a real plot, a real direction, a real date — not from adjectives.

Do

"Your plot faces north-east — strong for the kitchen, and a good start for the whole layout."
"Foundation work is complete. Walls begin Monday, weather holding."
"K-Smart approval filed on the 4th — typical clearance is 15 working days."

Don't

"Unlock cosmic prosperity energies for your dream home journey!"
"Everything's going great, don't worry about anything!"
"Approval is in process, we'll let you know eventually."
