The A-Z Workflow

A complete walk-through from the first compass reading to the final key handover. We account for every direction, every step of the way.

PHASE 01

The Reading
(Vastu Consulting)

We begin on site. Establishing the exact compass orientations, assessing natural elements, and plotting the Vastu Purusha Mandala over the raw land. This ensures the foundational geometry aligns with absolute precision before a single brick is drawn.

PHASE 02

The Blueprint
(Vastu Drawings & K-Smart)

The grid dictates the floor plan. We translate the initial reading into compliant architectural drawings—placing fire elements in the Southeast and maximizing air flow in the Northwest. We then handle all local K-Smart approvals, ensuring the spatial vision is legally cleared.

PHASE 03

The Build
(Construction)

Translating paper to ground. Using rich, local materials like laterite and teak, our site supervisors manage the build day-to-day. We ensure contractors don't compromise the meticulous alignments established in Phase 01.

PHASE 04

The Handover
(Finished Space)

A completed home bathed in natural light, embodying serenity and balance. From the macro-geometry of the plot to the micro-placement of the interior finishes, the result is a beautiful space built to endure.
