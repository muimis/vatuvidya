## 2. About Us / Our Philosophy
**Headline:** Two Kinds of Alignment.
**Body:** 
At Vastu Vidya Homes, we believe in the power of Vastu Shastra—an ancient Indian system of architecture based on the principles of harmony and balance in the natural world. By aligning your home with the principles of Vastu, we create spaces conducive to positive energy and well-being. 

A house is aligned twice — once to the compass, once to the contractor's calendar. Most firms hand you one and leave you to chase the other. Vastu Vidya Homes holds both lines at once. The same team that reads your plot's geometry stays on site until the keys are in your hand.

## 3. Our Area of Expertise (Services)
*Note: I have combined the live site's copy with the new service pillars defined in the Brand Guide.*

- **Vastu Consulting & Architecture**
  Our team of Vastu Vidya experts specializes in designing and building homes that are in harmony with the natural elements and energy flow. We speak in measurements, not mysticism.
- **Vastu Drawings & Interior Design**
  We provide a holistic approach, taking into account the direction of the space, the use of colors, and natural materials (laterite, teak) to create a balanced design.
- **K-Smart Approval & Compliance**
  Local-body permit clearance handled end-to-end, because every Vastu reading must tie to a compliant drawing and approval.
- **Construction & Outdoor/Garden**
  Site execution and project management from the grid to the handover. Includes garden design, landscape architecture, and outdoor construction.
- **Renovation**
  Additions, retrofits, and re-alignment of existing spaces to correct energy flows.
